02 · The Location

North Shore at the Landing — an established gated community minutes from Medical City

North Shore Village (marketed as The Landings at North Shore) is an established gated enclave off Boggy Creek Road, just west of Narcoossee Road in the Boggy Creek corridor of southeast Orlando / Osceola County. The community was built between 2006 and 2011 by Centerline Homes and D.R. Horton, giving it mature landscaping and a settled, residential feel that newer subdivisions cannot replicate.

The community sits directly in the Lake Nona / Medical City growth corridor — a meaningful draw for the relocation, physician, and corporate renter profiles that anchor this market. Its gated entry, mature streetscape, and pond-and-fountain water features give it a settled, residential character that the newer subdivisions nearby cannot replicate, and the subject's lot sits on the water for the best of that setting.

Reading 4454 Indigo Sky — the "two leases" explained

4454 Indigo Sky shows two recorded leases three weeks apart (04/08 then 04/28), both at $3,300 / 100% of ask. The close dates make the pattern clear: the first lease almost certainly broke before move-in, and the property re-leased almost immediately. This is not two separate market validations — it is one five-bedroom property that proved durable $3,300 demand and re-let in under two weeks when the first applicant fell through. We treat it as a single, strong data point at the five-bedroom tier.

How the subject adjusts against each comp

vs. 4466 Indigo Sky ($3,000, leased in 36 days): The subject wins on size (+250 sq ft), water view, three-car garage, and the pond-view lot. It clearly outranks this property — which supports a premium over $3,000. But note it took 36 days to lease even at $3,000, our first signal that the four-bedroom buyer here is price-sensitive on speed.

vs. 4475 Biscayne Breeze ($3,100, still sitting at 54+ days): This is the closest feature match — it also has a water view and screened lanai — and the subject still beats it on square footage, a full third bathroom, and the three-car garage. This is the most important number on the page: a four-bedroom with the same headline features as ours has been unable to lease at $3,100 in nearly eight weeks. The four-bedroom market has not validated $3,100 for speed.

vs. 4454 Indigo Sky ($3,300, leased fast): On a pure feature grid the subject nearly matches it — losing the fifth bedroom but gaining the water view, screened lanai, 250 sq ft, and three-car garage. The lesson here isn't "price at $3,300" — it's that renters shop by bedroom count first. A family searching "5 bed" never sees our four-bedroom, and a family searching "4 bed" compares us to the $3,000–$3,100 field, not to this listing.

The line we do not cross

$3,300 is five-bedroom territory. The only property that achieved it has a fifth bedroom and re-let fast because of it. A four-bedroom listed at $3,300 invites the exact 54-day outcome 4475 is living right now — and a long vacancy erases the premium many times over (see below).

Recommended launch rent
$3,195 / month
List at $3,195 with a day-10 checkpoint. If we do not have a qualified application in hand by day 10, reduce to $3,150 to protect the 21-day target. This keeps us at the top of the four-bedroom market while guaranteeing we never drift into the stall zone.
05 · The 21-Day Plan

How we convert a premium property into a fast, qualified lease

Lead with the water

The hero photo is the pond view from the screened lanai. It is our single biggest click-and-showing driver and the one feature the leased $3,000 comp could not offer.

Price sharp from day one

Launch at $3,195 — not $3,250 "to test." The first 10 days carry the most search visibility and the freshest traffic; a sharp launch captures it before the listing ages.

Frame the lifestyle, qualify hard

Market the gated community, pond-view lanai, and Medical City proximity to the relocation / corporate renter, then run full screening so a fast lease is also a clean one. Speed and tenant quality are not a trade-off when we price correctly.

Day-10 checkpoint

No qualified application by day 10 → reduce to $3,150. This single pre-agreed move is what keeps us off 4475's 54-day path.
